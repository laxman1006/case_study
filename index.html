Design & Engineering

The challenge

The team at Emtax envisioned to build an automated solution for tax compliance, after GCC's new VAT legislation was announced.

Product Design Goal

The goal was to build something extremely modular scalability would be at its core to essentially drive adoption.

Achieving perfection with the technical design was key given the complexity of the problem and the volume of data that would get processed.

Users needn’t a learning curve to derive value from a product. We focused on making the UX extremely simple; with minimal effort, the user would get what they need.

Iterations improved the flow and brought it down to ‘Initiate a Return’, ‘Upload Batches of Data’, ‘Run Tax Engine’, and ‘Download Tax Return’.

Forty different things under the hood drove the engine, the UX tucking them all.

What we ended up with: a well-crafted master data warehouse that allows users to set themselves up. The validation engine uncluttered the data flow and helped users identify errors as well.

With that, the tax engine allowed one to configure their setup as per their country’s law and consolidated complex data into comprehensible reports.
